Room Facilities

Our rooms are designed for ultimate comfort and relaxation, featuring spacious interiors with modern amenities and eco-friendly touches. Each room includes cozy beds, air conditioning and private balconies offering stunning views. Enjoy hot water showers in our stylish attached bathrooms with complimentary eco-toiletries. Select rooms provide outdoor seating or hammocks for a closer connection to nature. With solar-powered energy and sustainable practices, we offer a luxurious yet environmentally conscious stay.
